This website has been created for the project "This could be happening here", in the context of the 48H Neukoelln festival 2020 by the Berlin-based feminist collective Trial and Theresa. It illustrates the beginning of a research around the concept of surveillance, a term whose meaning constant oscillations between notions of care and control resonate strongly in today's news.

If history has thought us something, it is that the closer to you the evil sets up, the slower you are to notice. Stay alert!
